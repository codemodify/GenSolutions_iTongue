iTongue – Point. Tap. Translate.


Translate from 1 language (Spanish) to 53 languages by just pointing your camera to the text you want to be translated then move, resize and zoom the magic rectangle to grab the area you’re interested in. Use special modes for travel, reading books, newspapers or restaurant menus. Also use Text Mode for manual translations. 


4 Modes To Make It Easy


Travel Mode – use it every day whenever you’re traveling or not, move and resize the text recognition rectangle wherever  you need with your finger. Zoom in if you’re not close to the subject.

Book Mode – useful for quick translations while reading books or restaurant menus. Translate a word, a line or a whole paragraph.

Text Mode – for times when you don’t have an image. Just type your text and select the language – we've got you covered.

Scanner Mode – scan a document and send it to your email for further use.


Online & Offline


Whether you have an internet connection or not iTongue will work because it comes with a set of offline dictionaries for the following languages: English, Chinese, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Portuguese, Russian, Spanish.
