Book Mode Help 

Choose what you need to be translated by pressing one of the "W", "L", "P" buttons in the bottom-left corner. W – for a word or two. L – for a line. P – for a paragraph.

Press the camera icon when you’re ready and it will recognize the text and translate it. 

Change the language by pressing on the flag at the top.

Enable flashlight if you don’t have enough light in the top-right corner.
